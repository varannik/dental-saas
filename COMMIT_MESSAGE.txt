## Technology Recommendations

- ASR: Deepgram Nova-3 (streaming, medical vocabulary)
- LLM: GPT-4.1 + Claude multi-model router
- Agent: LangGraph (confirmed best choice for HITL workflows)
- ORM: Drizzle (pgvector support, multi-tenant friendly)
- Frontend: Next.js 15 + shadcn/ui + Tailwind v4
- Container: ECS Fargate (MVP) → EKS (scale)
- Database: Aurora Serverless v2 (production)
- Observability: OpenTelemetry + LangSmith
